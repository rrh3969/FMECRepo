Fundraisers

Hot Dog Roast

Every Saturday between May and Late October you can stop by the Exhibit Center to grab some lunch. We serve up our famous hot dogs from 10am-2pm. Our Happy Hotdoggers are out rain or shine!

Memorial Tree Raffle

Our Memorial Tree Raffle runs during the summer months each year. Your raffle ticket earns you a chance to win a "tree's worth" of cash, lottery tickets, and gift cards. Drawing occurs Labor Day Weekend. You can pick up a ticket during our Saturday Hot Dog Roast.

6-Month Lotto

Each year we run two 6-Month Lotteries. The first runs January 1st - June 30th and the second runs July 1st - December 31st. Tickets are available through our members or stop by on Saturdays to pick one up. Prizes are drawn for each day with larger amounts for Sundays and Holidays.

Past Events

Greater Buffalo Fire Muster

On June 24,th 2023, the Firemen's Memorial Exhibit Center had the pleasure of hosting the Genesee Valley Chapter of the Society for the Preservation & Appreciation of Antique Motor Fire Apparatus in America (SPAAMFAA) for their second annual Greater Buffalo Fire Muster. Several pieces of antiques motorized fire apparatus were on display. Even our local industry expert, Dick Young of Young Fire Equipment and Performance Advantage Company, stopped in to take in the sights and impart just a little of his vast knowledge on the subject.
